# Evaluating AI-Assisted Decision Support During Simulated Mars Missions

## Research Overview

Future Mars missions will require astronauts to make critical decisions in environments where communication with Earth is delayed and immediate support is limited.

This research explores how Artificial Intelligence (AI) can assist astronauts by providing decision support during simulated Mars mission scenarios.

The goal is to evaluate whether AI assistance improves decision-making speed, accuracy, confidence, and overall mission performance.

---

## Research Question

Does AI-assisted decision support improve human decision-making during simulated Mars missions?

---

## Hypothesis

Participants who use AI-assisted decision support will make faster and more accurate decisions compared to participants who do not use AI assistance.

---

## Research Objectives

1. Evaluate the effectiveness of AI recommendations during mission emergencies.
2. Measure decision-making speed with and without AI assistance.
3. Analyze user trust and confidence when interacting with AI.
4. Understand the benefits and limitations of AI in space exploration environments.
